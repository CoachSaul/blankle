How to play

BL_NKLE is a daily German fill-in-the-blank puzzle. Each day four new puzzles are published, one for each level.

Our levels roughly align with the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR):

  • Beginner (A1): simple present tense, everyday vocabulary
  • Easy (A2): past tense and connectors, familiar situations
  • Medium (B1): mixed tenses, subordinate clauses, varied vocabulary
  • Hard (B2): complex grammar, abstract topics, rich vocabulary

Fill in the gaps using the word bank below the story.

Check out new puzzles daily to keep your streak alive. Share your score with friends and try to get that perfect BL_NKLE.
