How to Play

  1. Read the story. Each blank is numbered and marked with a line.
  2. Type your answer into each blank. The word bank shows you which words to use.
  3. The word bank shows words differently depending on your level:
    • Beginner: type the exact word shown
    • Easy: verbs appear as infinitives, conjugate them correctly
    • Medium and Hard: all words must be correctly inflected or declined
  4. Press Check answers when you are ready to see your score.
  5. A new puzzle drops every day at four levels: Beginner, Easy, Medium and Hard.
  6. Share your score with friends and try to get that perfect BL_NKLE.
